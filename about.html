About Us

The Wesean Student Federation (WSF) is headquartered in Shillong, a symbolic site as the former capital of the Northeast Presidency under British administration. Our federation strives to empower students through advocacy, education, and leadership across the Wesea region.

Regions Served

  • Northeast India, including the Gorkha and Koch areas of West Bengal
  • Bangladesh: Chittagong, Sylhet, Mymensingh
  • Myanmar: Kachin-Chin and Naga areas

History of the Wesean Student Federation

The Wesean Student Federation was created by Rajen Monseng Borborua, an Ahom high schooler from Tezpur. With mentorship and guidance from Luhng Kokai, this website serves as a platform to unite and empower students across Wesea.
